Biome Creator

üí° How to use

This tool will help create an automatic custom biome JSON output that can be copied into a datapack and loaded into worlds. Before clicking the "Download" button at the bottom of the page be sure to click the "Generate" button and review your output.

Elements like the temperature and downfall are float values and are specified between 0 and 1 using decimals.

  • Temperature: Controls gameplay features like grass and foliage color and whether snow golems take damage.
  • Downfall: Controls grass and foliage color, a value above 0.85 also makes fire burn out faster.
  • Grass Colour Modifier: Controls grass colour variation based on some vanilla biomes.

üå≥ Colours

You can set full RGB custom colour values for the sky, sky fog, water, water fog, grass, and foliage. These colour values are applied on top of a greyscale image so expect some colour offset with regards to grass/water vibrancy when viewed in-game.

‚ú® Particles

Biomes can emit client side particles in 3D world space based on the values you enter here but it is optional and not required for the biome or this tool to work. When selecting a particle you can choose from the dropdown menu and enter the probability of that particle appearing.
These particles will be ambiently emitted in all air spaces in a 3D biome.

The probability controls how often the particle spawns, a value of 1 (the highest value) will be a LOT of particles. Good amient values are ususally between 0.01 and 0.05 but you can go higher or lower if you want. The bounds are capped at 0 to 1.

üéµ Sounds

Theres not much here yet but theres an ambient sound that will play client side occasionally for players in the custom biome. In future this will support mood sound effects, ambient music, and addition sounds.
